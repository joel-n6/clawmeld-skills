# ClawMeld Positioning Options

Do not default to "voice notes app." That is too weak.

## Option 1: Founder operating system
**Promise:** Turn voice thoughts into decisions, tasks, and research briefs.

Pros:
- strong daily frequency
- founder pain is real
- fits OpenClaw automation well

Risks:
- broad ICP if not narrowed
- founders may churn if not embedded into workflow

## Option 2: Creator content engine
**Promise:** Turn spoken thoughts into publish-ready posts, threads, and outlines.

Pros:
- easy to demo
- fast time-to-value
- good shareability

Risks:
- very crowded market
- may need strong editorial quality to justify price

## Option 3: Consultant / client follow-up assistant
**Promise:** Turn field notes and voice captures into client-ready summaries and next steps.

Pros:
- clear ROI
- higher willingness to pay
- easier path to B2B-lite pricing

Risks:
- narrower market
- needs strong structured outputs and trust

## Option 4: Sales note to CRM update
**Promise:** Turn spoken sales notes into structured CRM entries and follow-ups.

Pros:
- direct revenue tie
- high frequency
- business budget exists

Risks:
- integration-heavy
- incumbents already exist

## Option 5: AutoResearch assistant
**Promise:** Capture a thought and receive a research brief with risks, options, and sources.

Pros:
- distinctive
- aligned with current product direction
- good premium story

Risks:
- expensive to deliver if overused
- may be too broad without a clear persona

## Selection rule

Prefer the option with:
1. strongest willingness to pay
2. fastest visible value
3. clearest distribution story
4. best differentiation from commodity note apps
